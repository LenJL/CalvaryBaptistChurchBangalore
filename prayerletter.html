Prayer Letter

Dear Pastors, Brothers and Friends in Christ,

Greetings to you all in the mighty name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ!


We are reaching out to share a special prayer request for the Ministry the Lord has laid in our heart to the local communities in Ooty District, Tamil Nadu State in India.

Our vision is to reach out to every individual with the transformative Gospel and love of our Lord Jesus Christ regardless of their background and faith affiliations. This has been possible through Education sponsorship and support for the underprivileged Children, support for Widows and the Elderly as we minister the Gospel amongst the communities.

Through the Ministry outreach, the support programs, and spiritual guidance, a greater population in these communities have begun to see the impact of the love of Jesus Christ in their lives.

Due to the enormity of the task before us, we want to humbly ask for your prayers and support to fund the various initiatives, including educational programs for the Children, healthcare initiatives, community development, support for the elderly and the widows.

On behalf of the Ministry, we also want to extend a warm invitation to witness firsthand the transformative work we are doing in the Communities.

We believe that seeing the direct impact of your generous support will be an incredibly rewarding experience, with it you will also have the privilege to meet the beneficiaries, Connect with the individuals and families whose lives have been greatly impacted by your prayers and contributions.

We understand how valuable time may be. We therefore are open to flexible visit schedules that suits your convenience. Please let us know your availability, and we will be happy to arrange a personalized visit itinerary.

We sincerely appreciate your partnership and look forward to every opportunity as we share in this noble work of transforming more and more lives in the local Communities of Masinagudi and surrounding villages in Ooty District, Tamil Nadu State.

Thank you once again for partnering with us in prayer and funding programs. Together, we can make a difference to these precious souls.



Blessings,

In His Service,

Pastor Ruben Paul,
Calvary Baptist Church, Bangalore, India.

Pastor Isaiah Okeyo,
Rhema Celebration Centre, Dubai.
